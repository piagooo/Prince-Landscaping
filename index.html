Prince Landscaping

With over 10 years of experience in Phoenix, Prince Landscape is a trusted name in professional landscape design and installation. We specialize in hardscaping, turf installation, stonework, planting, and custom fire features, delivering tailored solutions that enhance outdoor living spaces of all sizes.

At Prince Landscape, we pride ourselves on working closely with each client to understand their unique vision and space requirements. Whether you're designing a landscape for a new build or refreshing an existing yard, we are committed to helping you make the most of your space—no matter the lot size.

From elegant stone patios to vibrant plant layouts and functional fire elements, our team brings creativity, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail to every project. Let Prince Landscape turn your outdoor space into a personalized oasis you’ll enjoy for years to come.
